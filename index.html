I'm Anuprita Dhamale — a Data & BI Engineer based in San Francisco Bay Area, with a Master's in Information Systems from Northeastern University. I specialize in building the full data layer: from ETL pipelines and cloud warehouses to the dashboards and AI-powered insights that sit on top.

At American Choice Healthcare, I architect pipelines that process 100M+ records weekly across Snowflake, AWS Redshift, and Tableau — with a focus on making data accessible and actionable, including integrating LLMs (OpenAI API) into dashboards to automate narrative reporting and cut manual effort by 50%.

The work goes beyond the pipeline. Understanding the business problem, choosing the right data model, instrumenting quality checks, and delivering something a non-technical executive can actually use — that's where the real value is.

My background spans Data Engineer, BI Engineer, and Data Analyst work — and the kind of problems I find most interesting live at companies where data is a core competitive advantage, not an afterthought.
